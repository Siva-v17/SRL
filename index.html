/ 01 — Who we are

A laboratory built for questions, not quarters.

Founded by a team of displaced academics who were tired of writing grants instead of running experiments, Stackly operates with the discipline of a research university and the speed of a startup. No tenure committees. No publish-or-perish. Just rigorous, reproducible science.

/ 04 — How a study moves through the lab

From hunch to published fact.

STEP 01

Hypothesis & Design

Researchers draft a falsifiable hypothesis and pre-register the experimental design before a single sample is touched.

STEP 02

Instrumentation

Our fabrication shop builds bespoke rigs and sensors when off-the-shelf instruments aren't precise enough.

STEP 03

Trial & Replication

Every result is run by a second, blind team before it's allowed anywhere near a manuscript.

STEP 04

Publish & Open Data

Raw datasets, code and negative results ship alongside the paper — failures are findings too.
