/ Quick answers

Before you submit.

How fast will I hear back?

Two business days for a scoping call, regardless of project size. Urgent reproducibility requests are flagged and answered same-day.

Do you sign NDAs before a scoping call?

Yes — send your standard NDA with the intake form, or use ours, which our legal team can turn around within 24 hours.

Can I tour the facility?

We run facility tours for active partners and prospective hires on the first Friday of every month. Ask your scoping-call contact to add you.
