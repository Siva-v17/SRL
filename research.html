/ 01 — Focus areas

Four questions we're currently obsessed with.

Genomics

Can gene-editing precision be decoupled from delivery efficiency?

A four-year program testing whether the real bottleneck in therapeutic editing is targeting, not the editor itself.

Materials

What's the real ceiling on room-temperature superconductivity?

Independent replication of six high-profile claims, with full instrumentation logs published either way.

Climate

Are current ocean carbon-sink models underestimating microbial uptake?

A 40,000-sensor deployment feeding a model that's already disagreeing with three published forecasts.

Computation

Can a digital twin predict material fatigue before physical testing can?

Training simulation models against five years of our own physical stress-test archive.
