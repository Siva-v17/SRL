/ Mission

Independent science, owned by no one but the data.

We exist to run the studies that universities can't fund and companies can't risk: long-horizon, high-failure-rate research with no guaranteed commercial payoff. Roughly a third of our work never gets published as a positive result — and we publish it anyway.

Stackly is structured as a public-benefit research trust. No shareholders dictate our roadmap; an independent science board does.

/ Operating principles

What we won't compromise on.

①

Pre-registration, always

Every hypothesis is timestamped and filed before data collection begins — no retrofitting a story onto results.

②

Negative results ship too

A study that disproves a hypothesis is published with the same rigor as one that confirms it.

③

Open data by default

Raw datasets and analysis code accompany every paper unless a partner contract specifically restricts it.
